About Us

Connecting style and technology since 2022

Who Are We?

At Stylo Tech, we merge urban fashion with innovative technology. We believe that clothing should not only look great but also serve a smart and practical purpose in your daily life.

Founded in 2022, we are pioneers in developing smart and sustainable apparel.

Our headquarters are located in Yucatán, Mexico. We proudly work with 100% Mexican designers and love showcasing traditional Yucatecan clothing for both men and women—so everyone can find the garment that suits them best.

We are passionate about innovation and always strive to release new pieces in our stores.
